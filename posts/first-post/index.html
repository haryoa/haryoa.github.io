My Warm Welcome!

By Haryo A W
Posted 2022-04-15 3 min read

Warm Welcome from me!

Random Anya

Welcome, everyone! Finally, my personal blog has been created 😊😊😊😊! I’m happy that my plan of having a personal blog is realized! Phew…

It’s been a long time since I haven’t written any new posts. Well, let me open up that I actually want to write something on the past. But, yeah a lot of stuff happened, and it had been neglected. When I came back to write something, I felt lost in motivation to write anything or I should say procrastinating 🫠. After a long time, finally I won fighting it and as the result, I decided to create this website.

Introduction

Okay, for you that don’t know me (well, I’m sure you do! I’m not famous 😭), Let me introduce myself. My name is Haryo Akbarianto Wibowo. You can call me “Haryo” here. I’m an AI enthusiast that has experience in the AI industry as an Artificial Intelligence (AI) engineer or researcher. I have an interest in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision (CV). I’m here to share my knowledge, especially about AI in text. Sometimes, I will write about personal development and life experience.

For your information, I used to write in my Medium. I decided to move here because I can customize what I need in this website ( I have freedom to change my website layout and content :D). There are some personal projects that I have done such as AnimeGAN (abomination GAN XD), AI game, and music generator. If you are interested, feel free to visit my Medium~.

I also published several academic papers in the NLP field. My papers are about colloquial problems (with machine translation), model compression, and analysis with the smol model. Feel free to see my papers here if you are interested.

Why do I write?

Another Random Anya

I write these posts as the process of learning something. I believe that when you share knowledge, it will accelerate your learning process. When you write, it’s also a process to make you understand yourself. It will make you think to organize what you know and pour them into the blog. From my experience, I really have benefited a lot by writing and sharing some useful kinds of stuff. For me, I’m very happy just having my post benefit you 😄.

In my AI posts, I mostly do some random AI projects. I do it just for fun. If anyone wants to do a fun thing together, feel free to discuss it with me through email or Linkedin. If I don’t reply to you, sometimes I accidentally skip it. You can reach out again.

I plan to post some ML academic research summaries in the future. Many things happen in the ML fields and I want to make a simple post about it. Just think of it as my practice in doing summarization~.

Learning Process

Uhmm.. yep, I said it about writing posts as the process of learning. Sometimes, I make mistakes. Feel free to discuss in the article’s comment section if you find something wrong in it. I enjoy feedback to make me a better person, so feel free to leave some feedback on my site.

Closing Thoughts

Hope you have fun with my blog~

This post will be updated in the future. This blog’s name and tagline may change in the future.
